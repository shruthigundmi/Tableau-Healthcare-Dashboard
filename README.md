# Tableau-Healthcare-Dashboard
This is my academic project cpmpleted in August 2025 during MBA.

# Dashboard 1 - SLEEP INSIGHTS
This dashboard provides a complete sleep analysis by showcasing sleep efficiency based on sleep routine consistency and diet type, the impact of device use and screen time on sleep efficiency, sleep duration across different occupations, and average sleep quality by chronotype, helping to understand sleep patterns and support healthier lifestyle decisions.

# Dashboard 2 - HEALTH & LIFESTYLE ANALYSIS 
This dashboard offers insights into health and lifestyle patterns by analyzing heart rate and blood pressure by gender, occupation-wise work hours, mental health trends across different work schedules, physical activity levels by BMI category, and the relationship between water and caffeine intake across diet types, enabling better wellness awareness and healthier decision-making.
